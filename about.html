About ZipZap

The Start

Founded three years ago, ZipZap was created with one clear aim — to provide a friendly and reliable removals service people and businesses in Edinburgh can depend on. What began as an idea between two friends has grown into a trusted operation built on consistency, hard work and attention to detail.

The Mission

Our mission is to deliver stress-free moving and transport services through efficiency, clear communication and reliable execution. Every move is handled with the same commitment to professionalism whether working with homeowners or business clients.

The Reason

Clients choose ZipZap for punctuality, strong work ethic and consistent service standards. With experience across residential and commercial work, we deliver dependable results regardless of the size or complexity of the job.
